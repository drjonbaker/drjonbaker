## Jonathan Baker, Ph.D

Cycling coach and exercise physiologist. I run [Palmares](https://palmares.co.uk) — structured training plans for time-limited cyclists, built on sports science and a decade in professional cycling.

### What I do

- **[Palmares](https://palmares.co.uk)** — training plans for cyclists who have careers, families, and limited hours to train. Sixteen plans differentiated by age, ability, and weekly hours, delivered through TrainingPeaks.
- **Data Scientist at Alpecin Premier Tech** — UCI WorldTour team. Race analytics, performance modelling, rider development.
- **Research** — exercise physiology, endurance performance, training prescription. 231+ citations.

Training prescription for the 5–12 hours-per-week rider. The gap between what the literature says and what most training plans actually do. Carbohydrate fuelling for endurance performance. How to build training that educates athletes rather than making them coach-dependent.
